Somewhere Between Equations & Flying Robots

I'm an Aerospace Systems Engineering student with a strong interest in software development, autonomy and aerospace systems. Right now, I'm part of a team developing a UAV platform with onboard AI for object detection and accompanying software. It's given me the chance to dive into computer vision, flight control, and systems integration - and shown me how much I enjoy solving messy, real-world engineering problems.

Outside of the tech, I've learned the value of persistence and adaptability. Balancing study, projects, and work has shaped how I approach challenges - and as the first in my family to pursue higher education, I'm motivated to keep pushing forward. My goal is to keep contributing to aerospace projects - whether that's smarter UAVs or bigger systems that support Australia's platforms - anywhere I can combine my engineering foundations with software and systems thinking to make something useful.
